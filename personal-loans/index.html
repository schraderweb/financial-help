How To Get A Personal Loan Even If Your Credit Is Poor

March 24, 2015November 13, 2018 admnh-hfxz 0 Comments
If you have poor credit you might think it’s impossible to get approved for a personal loan. The truth is you can get approved for a personal loan if you know where to look.
Here are 3 personal loan sites that accept anyone no matter what their credit situation is.

1. ZippyLoans

Zippy loans is a popular personal loan website that has been in business for over 8 years and is the #1 personal loan website for people looking for loans for anywhere from $100 to $15,000.

The requirements for Zippyloan are quite simple. The only requirements to get a loan from zippyloan are:

  1. Name, Address, Amount you would like to borrow.
  2. What kind of job you have (you don’t have to put employer info)
  3. Banking info so they can direct deposit your money.

That’s it. The qualifying process only takes 5 minutes and you can get money in your bank account as soon as tomorrow.

The repayment options offered by zippyloan are also very generous. For example: If you had a credit rating of 450, you could request a loan of $2000 with a repayment plan of 24 months. Your interest rate on that loan would be around 24% and your monthly payments would be around $118. Not bad considering most payday loan places have interest rates as high as 70% or more!

Zippyloan’s customer service is also top rated. If you happen to be late on a payment you can simply call them and request an extension so you aren’t hit with big late fees like other personal loan companies.

If you need to take out a personal loan ZippyLoans is your best bet if you have less than great credit, with great interest rates and repayment options.

To see if you qualify for a loan from Zippyloans click here

 

2. CashUSA.com

CashUSA is another great personal loan company that deals with smaller amounts. If you need a loan for less than $1000 CashUSA is your go to site.

Their interest rates and repayment plans aren’t as great as ZippyLoan, but this is a great second pick if you need a loan for under $1000.

Here are the requirements for CashUSA

  1. Name, Address, Amount you would like to borrow.
  2. What kind of job you have.
  3. Banking info so they can direct deposit your money.

To see if you qualify for a loan at CashUSA click here.

 

3. SpeedyNetLoan

If all else fails and for some reason you aren’t approved at one of the above websites, your best shot at getting approved for a personal loan is SpeedyNetLoan. They will approve you even if your credit is beyond destroyed, however you will have to deal with higher interest rates and shorter repayment options.

Here are the requirements to qualify for a loan on SpeedyNetLoan:

  1. Name, Address, Amount you would like to borrow.
  2. What kind of job you have.
  3. Banking info so they can direct deposit your money.

Click here to apply for a loan at SpeedyNetLoan

This should be your last resort if you absolutely need cash now. Avoid any other payday loan services if possible and stick to these 3 websites listed here unless you want to deal with insane interest rates and high monthly payments.
